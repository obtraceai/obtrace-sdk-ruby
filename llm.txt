Obtrace SDK Ruby

Purpose:
- Ruby backend SDK for logs, metrics, spans, propagation, and Rack/Rails helper baseline.

Core modules:
- obtrace_sdk/client
- obtrace_sdk/context
- obtrace_sdk/otlp
- obtrace_sdk/framework

Design principle:
- Thin SDK, no business rule authority.
- Backend/ingest/control-plane is source of truth for policy and business logic.
